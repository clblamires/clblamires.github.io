About Me

Hi, I'm Casey! I'm a web designer and developer. I've been working on the web for over 15 years and am currently a program advisor and professor for the Web Design and Development program at Lewis-Clark State College. I got started in this industry following the development of an online message board for a retro gaming website. I began writing themes and plugins for the board.

I eventually began writing websites for friends and family, including the now defunct website of my old high school. Once I graduated I began working for clients and customers in the region. I graduated from LCSC with my Bachelor's in Web Development with minor in Computer Science and Spanish. I currently study Human Resource Development at Idaho State UNiversity.

My wife and I live in Lewiston with our dog and cats. I like to draw, play games (board, video, card, or mind), computer things, playing music, gardening, painting, and cooking. Building things and solving puzzles are my passion!
